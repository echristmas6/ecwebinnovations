Tabletop Simulator

Tabletop Simulator is a web application designed as a digital gaming platform. The project features a selection of tabletop and casual games including Connect Four, Blackjack, and Cookie Clicker, all accessible through a centralized interface. Key features include user authentication (login/signup), and a focus on creating a lightweight, enjoyable, and easy-to-navigate online gaming experience with a rustic wood-themed visual style.

Studdy

Studdy is a web-based personalized study buddy built with Django and Python, emphasizing affordable and accessible learning resources. Utilizing the ChatGPT API, it generates tailored practice problems, making it ideal for self-directed high school students. Deployed on PythonAnywhere (a low-cost or free hosting platform), Studdy offers easily accessible quality learning material for grades 9-12. The website is designed to be easy to navigate with personalized questions and the flexibility of learning a variety of subjects from environmental science, biology, physical science, and many more.
